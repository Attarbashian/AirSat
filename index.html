AirSat

Satellite-Based Air Quality Monitoring Platform

Designed, developed, and implemented entirely by Vahid Attarbashian
(part of a broader PhD research project)

AirSat is a nationwide satellite-driven platform for visualizing and analyzing atmospheric pollutants across all regions of Iran. The system processes Sentinel-5P satellite data from the beginning of the mission up to the present and presents the results through fully interactive maps and analytical tools.
